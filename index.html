Don Roberts

Was Built in 2010

Don Roberts Elementary School is a highly rated, public school located in LITTLE ROCK, AR. It has 1,004 students in grades K-5 with a student-teacher ratio of 19 to 1. According to state test scores, 75% of students are at least proficient in math and 72% in reading.

has many rooms.

Don Roberts Elementary School has over 30 classrooms, a large cafeteria, a gym, 2 art rooms, 2 music rooms, a library, a counsler hall, 2 stories, ans much more.

Quiz Bowl Champs 2 years in a row
Last year, the Robets Quizbowl team got 1st and 3rd at the LRSD Quizbowl match. This year they score 1st and 2nd. They were also in the harder division this year.

Principal elected as Arkansas Elementary School Principal of the Year
Last year, Don Roberts Principal, Steven Helmick was elected as Arkansas Elementary School Principal of the Year. Steven Helmick will always remember the day he was announced Arkansas Elementary School Principal of the Year. Not just for the prestige of the award itself, which is considerable, or that it put him in the running for national honors.
